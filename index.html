Our Philosophy

At Harmony Junction, we believe that true recovery requires addressing the impact addiction has had on the mind, body, and spirit. We do that by combining the latest evidence-based approaches to recovery with complementary holistic therapies.

The result is a more balanced approach to recovery that helps you heal in more ways than a traditional program. This doesn’t just help you move on from drugs or alcohol. It also helps you establish an overall healthier way of being. Together, we’ll form a strong foundation that you can take with you to achieve your goals no matter where they take you next.

Our Name

The name Harmony Junction Recovery is reflective of our patients’ journeys. Struggling with an addiction to drugs or alcohol can leave you feeling like you’re wandering lost through your life. It’s a challenging path. But our junction is here to direct you toward the harmony you need to recover.

By the end of your time with us, you’ll be amazed at how far you have come. Instead of wandering through your life directionless, you’ll be right where you need to be, feeling pride and awe at the journey you took to get there.
